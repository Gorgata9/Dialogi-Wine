Located in the verdant heart of Guria, our vineyards are a sanctuary for Georgia's most elusive grapes. From the delicate rose notes of Chkhaveri to the deep character of Jani, our collection represents the pinnacle of biodiversity.

hkhaveri

The legendary rosé of Guria. Born in the mist-shrouded highlands, this rare indigenous variety offers a complex profile of wild berries, white peach, and spring flowers.

A delicate balance of vibrant acidity and soft tannins, capturing the true essence of Western Georgia. The wine showcases exceptional crispness with notes of red currant and pomegranate, evolving into subtle hints of rose petals and mineral undertones.

Tasting Notes: Wild strawberry, white peach, spring flowers, delicate tannins

Food Pairing: Grilled seafood, mild cheeses, Mediterranean cuisine
